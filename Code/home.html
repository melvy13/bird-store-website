About Mel's Birdies

Hello! I am Melvern Lim, the founder of Mel's Birdies, and I keep and sell pet birds. Birds, specifically budgies, are my favourite animals because these creatures are really cute, lovely and affectionate! I enjoy spending time with them as my hobby, and I hope many others share the same hobby as I do!

Here in Mel's Birdies, you can check out the online store where I sell pet birds products and accessories such as food and treats, cages, toys, grooming supplies and many more. For safety reasons, I only sell pet birds physically and not online.
If you need grooming or boarding services for your birds, I will be more than happy to help! Just book an appointment in advance using the form in the services page!
Any questions you want to ask about birds? Contact me using the form in the contact page!
