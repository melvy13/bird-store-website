Bird Grooming

Though birds do not need as much grooming as cats and dogs, that does not mean you can ignore it completely. Birds also require some grooming once in a while to keep them healthy, clean and pretty! With over 7 years of experience, I offer grooming services for all types of birds safely, while keeping them as relaxed and least stressful as possible. Book your appointment using the form below!

The four main types of grooming services that I provide are:

  • Beak Trimming

    • In truth, pet birds can naturally keep a healthy beak length by chewing foods and playing shredding toys. However, there's a chance the beaks can still overgrow and need some external help. In that case, never trim a bird's beak yourself unless you are a professional. Beak trimming is a complicated process, and doing so incorrectly can cause harm to the pet bird. In turn, it may affect your relationship with it.


  • Wing Trimming

    • Like beak trimming, wing trimming is also a rather complicated process and should be done by none other than a professional. Doing this process incorrectly could affect the bird's ability to fly safely and become injured.


  • Nail Trimming

    • Nail trimming helps keep your bird comfortable and also prevent your house (or yourself) from getting scratches. Bird's nails grow pretty fast and usually need to trimmed once every few months. If you want to trim it yourself, it is recommended that you get a helper to hold the bird for you, while you trim the nails.


  • Bird Bathing

    • Bathing your bird will keep it clean and hygenic. It also keeps their skin from being dry and itchy, which could lead to feather-plucking — a feather damaging behaviour. Birds only need baths once or twice a week, and if you plan to bath the bird yourself, be sure to use only clear plain water with a comfortable temperature. Bird Boarding

If you are going to be away from home on holidays, don't leave your bird in the cage! They will feel lonely and bored, while also not getting enough exercise. To keep your feathered companion healthy, I am happy to offer you bird boarding services and take care of your bird while you enjoy your time! I will provide necessary food while giving them sufficient play time, interaction and attention. Book your appointment using the form below!

If your bird was not purchased from this store, your bird will have to be tested for diseases before it can board with me. This is necessary to protect your bird and other birds around the area.
I will update you with photo of your feathered friend periodically while you are away, so you can continue to feel relaxed and enjoy your holiday without worrying too much!


You are advised to book an appointment at least 2 weeks in advance to avoid full bookings, especially during school holiday periods such as the summer break and end–of–year break, or public holidays such as Chinese New Year and Christmas. This is because I can only board about 10 birds at a time to allow every one of them to get enough attention everyday.
